Our Mission
At AdaptX, we are dedicated to making the web more accessible for everyone. Our platform is designed to support individuals with diverse needs, including dyslexia, color blindness, autism, and other disabilities. We provide innovative features that enhance digital accessibility, ensuring that everyone can navigate, read, and interact with content effortlessly.
